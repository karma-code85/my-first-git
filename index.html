Simple Omlette Recipe

an easy and quick dish ,perfect for any meal .this classic omelette combinesbeaten eggs cooked to perfect, optionally filled with your choice of these , vegetablesor meats

Preparation time

  • Total :Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • cooking : 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large ggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter oil
  • optinoal fillings : cheese. diced vegeablees, cooked meats herb

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a boe , beat the eggs with a pach of salt and pepper until they are will mixed .you can add atablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier textur
  2. Heat the pan : place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and butter oil
  3. cook the omelette: once the butter is melted and bubbing .pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the egges evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional): when the egges begin to set at the edges but are sitll slightly runny in the middle , sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelet
  5. Fold and serve : As the omelet continuse to cook , carefully lift one and fold it over the fillings , let for another minutes then slide it onto plat .
  6. Enjoy: serve hot , with additional salt and pepper if needed
